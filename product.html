TACKLE TOUGH TERRAIN IN LIGHT STABILITY

Run the trail in a super responsive running shoe. Fast and lightweight, it delivers a breathable and secure feel as you race over rocky paths. Updated traction lugs provide stability for your downhill miles.

Nike React technology is a lightweight and durable foam that delivers a responsive ride. A Zoom Air unit in the forefoot provides extra responsiveness, returning your energy to help guide you down the trail.

Multidirectional traction lugs in the heel are made from high-abrasion rubber. They help provide enhanced grip on descents and ascents.

A mesh and synthetic upper delivers a lightweight feel and enhanced ventilation.

A Dynamic Fit System through the midfoot helps hug and support your foot as your run through rocky trails.

More Benefits
  • Rock plate at the heel helps shield your foot on rough terrain.
  • Slim, cushioned collar provides soft comfort and a streamlined look.
  • Padded tongue helps relieve pressure caused by foot swelling over long mileage runs.
